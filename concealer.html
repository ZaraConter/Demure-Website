Usage Tips

  • Prep Your Skin: For optimal results, apply over a clean, moisturized face.
  • Apply Sparingly: Dispense a few drops onto your fingertips or use a foundation brush.
  • Blend Evenly: Starting at the center of your face, blend outwards for a seamless finish.
  • Build Coverage: Add layers to achieve your desired coverage without cakiness. For a dewy look, apply with a damp sponge.

Results

  • Glowing Finish: A radiant, naturally luminous complexion that enhances your skin’s natural beauty.
  • Hydrated Skin: Long-lasting hydration with a lightweight, breathable feel that doesn't clog pores.
  • Flawless Look: Evens skin tone, blurs imperfections, and offers buildable coverage for all-day wear.
  • Skincare Benefits: Skin feels smoother and looks healthier over time with regular use.

Composition

  • Hyaluronic Acid: Boosts moisture levels, plumping and smoothing skin for a hydrated finish.
  • Vitamin E: Protects against environmental damage while nourishing the skin.
  • Niacinamide: Helps even out skin tone and improve the appearance of fine lines.
  • Plant Extracts: Gentle botanical extracts soothe and protect sensitive skin.
  • Non-Comedogenic Formula: Free from pore-clogging ingredients, allowing skin to breathe naturally.
  • Dermatologically Tested: Safe for all skin types, including sensitive skin.
